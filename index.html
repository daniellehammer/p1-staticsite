Smith Island is an island located in Somerset County, Maryland. It sits in the Chesapeake Bay and faces rising sea levels as well as the direct effects of bay pollution! That's why the Chesapeake Bay Foundation works so hard to educate, research and provide solutions to the decreasing state of the bay.
